home photography printing video

Student at Millersville Universty

This project was to create a short-film about a topic relating to the Millersville campus. My group and I picked the printing press discribing how the machines function and a demonstration of a t-shirt printing process. We filmed this in Millersville Graphics department using the screen printing equipment. We did a step by step tutorial explaining how to use the ink and transfer through the mesh screen of the stencil print to the surface on to shirt.
